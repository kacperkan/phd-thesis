    \textbf{Additional qualitative examples (finest level of details) --}
    We report additional qualitative results of our method and the baselines.
    Empty images for FLoD~\cite{seo2024flod} denote subjects where the method
    failed to converge.
    However, it is worth noting that its quality is on par with
    OctreeGS~\cite{ren2024octree}, ScaffoldGS~\cite{lu2024scaffold} and
    3DGS~\cite{kerbl20233d} on all other samples.
    Please notice that ScaffoldGS~\cite{lu2024scaffold} and
    3DGS~\cite{kerbl20233d} produce artifacts for AVA (two last rows) which we
    argue come from insufficient coverage of cameras around the subjects and
    lack of the scale regularization mechanisms present in other approaches.
  }
